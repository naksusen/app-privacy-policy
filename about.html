Smart Budgeter

Our Story

Developed by Computer Science Students at Cavite State University - Bacoor Campus in 2024, Smart Budgeter is an innovative Android application designed to transform personal financial management through artificial intelligence.

Our Mission

To empower individuals with proactive, intelligent budgeting tools that adapt to changing economic conditions, enhance financial literacy, and support informed financial decision-making.

Key Features

  • Automatic Transaction Categorization
  • Personalized Budget Recommendations
  • Inflation Forecasting
  • Price Tracking for Essential Goods
  • Financial Education Resources

Technology

  • Platform: Android
  • Development: Flutter and Dart
  • Backend: Supabase
  • AI Capabilities: Machine Learning Algorithms

Our Commitment

Helping you navigate financial challenges with intelligent, personalized guidance.

Let Smart Budgeter take control of your financial future!
