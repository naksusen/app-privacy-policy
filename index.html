Privacy Policy

Last Updated: May 2025

Introduction

Welcome to Smart Budgeter, an Artificial Intelligence Android Application Budgeting Assistant designed to help you manage your personal finances proactively. We are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring the security of your personal information.

Information Collection and Use

Personal Data

Smart Budgeter collects and stores personal information such as your name, email address, and contact details to provide you with a personalized experience. This information is stored securely and is never shared with third parties without your explicit consent. All financial data you enter into the application remains securely on your device and is protected by our strict privacy controls.

User-Entered Financial Data

  • All financial data you input is stored locally on your device
  • The application does not transmit your financial information to external servers

Device Data

We may collect non-personal, anonymized device information for app performance improvement, including:

  • Device model
  • Operating system version
  • Country code
  • Anonymous crash reports

Data Sources

The application retrieves publicly available data from:

  • Philippine Statistics Authority
  • Bangko Sentral ng Pilipinas
  • Department of Agriculture
  • Trading Economics
  • Department of Energy

User Consent

By using Smart Budgeter, you agree to the terms of this privacy policy. If you do not accept these terms, please do not install or use the application.
